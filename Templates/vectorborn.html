Minor surgeries like cataract removal, tonsil removal, or similar procedures that require less than 24 hours of hospitalisation are covered under daycare procedures.

Pre-hospitalisation

Some surgeries or medical issues necessitate treatment before hospitalisation. The cost of such treatments can be claimed under health insurance as per the policy's terms and conditions.

Post-hospitalisation

The cost of follow-up treatment is covered under individual health insurance policies as per the policy's terms and conditions.

Domiciliary Treatment

The cost of receiving treatment at home can be covered under specific circumstances for a predetermined number of days.

Organ Donation

Newborn Baby Coverage

Daily Cash Benefit

This is generally an add-on cover. This amount can be used to buy medicines or other essentials required by you.

Intentional Injuries

The cost of treating intentional injuries, including attempted suicide, is excluded from the policy when you buy insurance.

Adventure Sports

Injuries arising from participating in adventure sports are not covered.

Criminal Activities

Medical attention required due to participating in criminal activity is not covered.

Cosmetic/Dental Surgeries

The cost of cosmetic and dental surgeries, unless required due to an accident, is excluded when you buy insurance.

Infertility Treatment

Mediclaim policy in India generally does not cover the cost of infertility treatment.

Not Mentioned in Policy

Any other medical treatments/surgeries that are not mentioned in the policy document will not be covered.

Select the right sum insured

Picking the right sum insured amount is super important. You want to make sure you're covered just right. So consider factors like your age, medical conditions, location, health insurance quote, and healthcare needs when choosing a plan. You want it to match your unique situation. Going for a plan with a low sum insured might leave you hanging when you need it the most during a medical emergency.

Your healthcare needs

Think about what you actually need from a healthcare policy in India. Do you have any pre-existing conditions or take regular medications? Knowing your healthcare needs will help you find a plan that covers the services and treatments you require. It's all about finding the perfect fit for you.

Network of healthcare providers

Hey, we all have our favourite doctors and hospitals, right? Well, check if the policy covers the ones you prefer. You want to make sure your primary care physician or specialists are in the network. And if you have a go-to hospital or medical centre, double-check that they're included too. This way it’s easier to have access to the ones you trust.

Premiums and deductibles

Let's talk about the money stuff. Medical insurance premiums are what you pay each month for your insurance, while deductibles are what you have to shell out of pocket before your coverage kicks in. Take a look at the full health insurance coverage cost and deductibles of different policies and find the one that fits your budget. It's about getting the coverage you need without breaking the bank.

Coverage limits and exclusions

You don't want any surprises down the road, right? So, make sure you understand the coverage limits and exclusions of the policy. Some plans may have limits on what they'll pay for certain treatments, or they may exclude certain conditions altogether. Knowing these details upfront will save you from any unexpected bumps in the road.

Additional benefits

Hey, let's talk about the cherry on top! Some policies offer extra goodies like wellness programs or mental health services. These additional benefits can really add value to your policy and make a big difference in your overall healthcare experience. So, think about what matters to you and your family, and look for a policy that offers those sweet extras.

Customer service and support

It's important to choose an insurance provider that's known for their stellar customer service. You want someone who's got your back when you need it most. Lifetime renewability

It is imperative to understand the time for which your medical plan is active. As health complications increase with age, there are high chances that you may require mediclaim policy in India during old age. High Claim Settlement Ratio (CSR)

High claim settlement ratios mean a higher probability that your claim may be settled. Select an insurer with a high CSR, as it reflects that it will not reject a claim without a valid reason.

Minimum waiting period

Many health plans come with a waiting period for different types of ailments. It is advisable to select a health policy with a lower waiting period for pre-existing conditions and diseases.
